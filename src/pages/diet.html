Fruits and vegetables: Eat a variety of fruits and vegetables, including whole fruits and not just apples and bananas.

Whole grains: Choose whole grains like whole wheat, brown rice, steel cut oats, and quinoa

Protein: Eat a variety of protein foods, including lean meats, poultry, seafood, eggs, legumes, nuts, and seeds

Healthy fats: Choose plant oils like olive oil and other vegetable oils. Nuts are a good source of healthy unsaturated fat

Fiber: Increase your dietary fiber intake to 25 to 30 grams a day

Drink water: Drink water or other beverages that are naturally calorie-free.

Reduce added sugars, sodium, and unhealthy fats: Reduce your intake of processed, refined, and ready-made foods that are high in sugar and fat

Minimally processed foods: Choose minimally processed, whole foods
